## MCQ 1
**Question:** What was unusual about the pears on the Shakespear tree?
A) They were poisonous.
B) They shimmered with a golden hue. 
C) They tasted like apples.
D) They grew upside down.
**Correct Answer:** B

## MCQ 2
**Question:**  What did the villagers believe about the Shakespear tree?
A) It brought bad luck.
B) It granted eternal life.
C) It held magical properties. 
D) It attracted dangerous animals.
**Correct Answer:** C

## MCQ 3
**Question:**  What inspired Emma to pursue her dream of becoming a writer?
A) The encouragement of her parents.
B) The visions she experienced after eating the Shakespear fruit. 
C) A traveling storyteller who visited Eldermere.
D) Winning a writing competition.
**Correct Answer:** B

## MCQ 4
**Question:**  How did Eldermere change after Emma started writing?
A) It became a ghost town.
B) It became a hub of storytelling. 
C) It became famous for its apple orchards.
D) It became a popular tourist destination for its architecture. 
**Correct Answer:** B

## MCQ 5
**Question:** What is the main theme of "The Shakespear Enigma"?
A) The importance of education.
B) The power of fear.
C) The beauty of nature.
D) The power of dreams and inspiration. 
**Correct Answer:** D
